Research & ISRU

AI-driven agromining for planetary soils

AgroCitrus combines imaging, AI soil intelligence and bio-derived bagasse sensors to scout mineral-rich zones and guide sampling—designed for low-mass, high-value planetary exploration missions.

Imaging & AI

Multispectral and RGB analysis with computer-vision models to classify regolith, detect alteration halos and estimate mineral prospectivity.

Depth Projection

Shallow geophysics + terrain analysis feed 3D models to project mineral likelihood at depth and prioritize sampling.

Bagasse Biosensors

Sugarcane bagasse–derived biosorbents (biochar/functionalized cellulose) bind trace metals for lightweight detection in drill cuttings or leachates.

About the solution

AgroCitrus is an integrated platform that fuses orbital and surface imagery with on-site biosensing to identify mineral signatures in planetary soils. Our AI models classify soil types and spectral fingerprints while a compact lab-on-chip uses treated bagasse cartridges to selectively bind and reveal trace metals. The system outputs ranked targets, sampling plans and uncertainty-aware guidance for rover operations and human teams.

Applications

  • ISRU scouting (metals, oxygen-bearing minerals)
  • Greenhouse site selection and water-ice detection
  • Low-mass biosensing for field validation of drill samples

Deliverables

  • Mineral probability maps & 3D depth estimates
  • Field kit: imaging workflow + biosorbent cartridges
  • On-device AI models for rover/drone deployment
